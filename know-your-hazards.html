Offline Survival Guide

Flood

  • Evacuate immediately if advised by LGUs.
  • Avoid wading in water (Leptospirosis risk).
  • Switch off main power & gas lines.
  • Secure valuables on high ground.

Landslide

  • Listen for rumbling or cracking trees.
  • Watch for new cracks in walls/floors.
  • Drop, Cover, and Hold if debris falls.
  • Avoid sleeping in landslide-prone areas during rain.

Storm Surge

  • Move to high ground far from coast.
  • Do not stay in beach-level structures.
  • Prepare a Go-Bag (food/water for 3 days).
  • Wait for official "All Clear" signal.
