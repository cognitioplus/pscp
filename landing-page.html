Detection

Multi-agency hazard monitoring with real-time alerting system integrated with PHIVOLCS, PAGASA, and NOAH APIs.

2

Diagnosis

ISO 31000-aligned risk analysis with AI-powered assessment of strategic, operational, financial, compliance, and environmental risks.

Monitoring
